Benefits of Membership

When you join UWISA, you gain access to comprehensive support and representation.

Legal Support & Representation

Full assistance with workplace disputes, unfair dismissals, and CCMA cases for both security and retail workers.

Collective Bargaining Power

A united voice for negotiating better pay, fair contracts, and safer workplaces.

Job Security Protection

Campaigns against casualisation and unfair retrenchments.

Training & Skills Development

Workshops, certifications, and capacity-building programs for both sectors.

Industry Updates

Regular news, labour law changes, and sector developments.

Networking & Solidarity

Connect with workers from different companies and build support systems.

Why We Include Retail Workers

Understanding the importance of retail workers in our union

We acknowledge the large workforce in retail and highlight the common challenges retail workers face: low wages, unpredictable schedules, unsafe environments, and unfair dismissals.

Retail workers are as essential as security officers. We currently have members from major retailers like Shoprite and are expanding to include workers from supermarkets, clothing stores, warehouses, and supply chains.

By combining retail and security workers, we multiply our bargaining power for better pay, job security, and dignity for all workers.
