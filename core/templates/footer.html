Privacy Policy

Effective Date: October 2025

1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following information when you use our website or apply for membership:

  • Full name, email address, phone number, and residential address
  • Employment details such as employer name, job title, and sector
  • Messages or inquiries you submit via our contact forms
  • Basic technical data (IP address, browser type, and usage patterns)

2. How We Use Your Information

Your information may be used to:

  • Process membership applications and maintain accurate records
  • Communicate with you regarding membership benefits, events, and services
  • Provide legal support, advice, and union representation
  • Improve our website and member experience

3. Sharing of Information

We will never sell or rent your personal data. We may only share your information with:

  • Authorized UWISA representatives for administrative purposes
  • Trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operations
  • Legal authorities if required by law

4. Data Security

We use technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute protection.

5. Your Rights

As a member or visitor, you have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request corrections or updates to your data
  • Withdraw consent for communications at any time
  • Request deletion of your data, subject to legal requirements

6. Cookies & Tracking

Our website may use cookies to remember preferences and improve user experience. You may disable cookies in your browser, but some features may not work properly.

7. Updates

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. We will post updates here with a new effective date.
