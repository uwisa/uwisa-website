About UWISA

The Union of Workers in the Security & Retail Industry of South Africa — your united voice fighting for fair wages, safe working conditions, and worker dignity.

Who We Are

UWISA is a united voice for workers in the security and retail sectors. We are more than just a union — we are a community of dedicated professionals who understand the challenges faced by workers in these essential industries.

We fight for fair wages, safe working conditions, and respect for worker dignity. Our union represents both security guards who protect our communities and retail staff who serve our customers every day.

From major retailers like Shoprite to independent security companies, we are expanding our reach to include workers from supermarkets, clothing stores, warehouses, and supply chains across South Africa.

Our Mission

To protect, empower, and advance the rights of security and retail workers. We ensure fair working conditions, legal representation, and collective bargaining power that reflects the voices of both sectors.

Our Vision

To become a powerful, legally recognized force defending and uplifting all security and retail workers in South Africa. To ensure no worker stands alone against injustice.

Our Values

The principles that guide our work and define our commitment to every member.

Worker Protection

We stand as a shield against unfair treatment, ensuring every worker is protected and their rights are respected.

Unity & Solidarity

Bringing together security and retail workers to amplify our collective voice and strengthen our bargaining power.

Dignity & Respect

Every worker deserves to be treated with dignity and respect, regardless of their role or position.

Professional Excellence

Supporting skill development and professional growth for all workers in our sectors.

Why We Include Retail Workers

We recognize the large workforce in retail and the common challenges they face: low wages, unpredictable schedules, unsafe environments, and unfair dismissals.

Retail workers are as essential as security officers. They are the backbone of our economy, serving customers and keeping our businesses running every single day.

We currently have members from major retailers like Shoprite and are expanding to include workers from supermarkets, clothing stores, warehouses, and supply chains across South Africa.

By combining retail and security workers, we multiply our bargaining power for better pay, job security, and dignity for all.

Our Commitment to You

"Whether you're guarding the gates or working the tills, UWISA is committed to fighting for your rights, your future, and your dignity. Together, we are stronger — and together, we will win."
